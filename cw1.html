Himalayas!

About the mighty Himalayas which touches the blue sky.

Mountain ranges in the Himalayas look very beautiful in summer with blue skies above it. The Himalayas stretch across the northeastern portion of India. They cover approximately 1,500 mi (2,400 km) and pass through the nations of India, Pakistan, Afghanistan, China, Bhutan and Nepal.
